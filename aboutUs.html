Our Story

What began in Dehradun over a decade ago as a small family-run service has today grown into Dinesh Caterers - trusted by 1000+ families and businesses across Uttarakhand and beyond. From 50-guest poojas to 2,000+ guest weddings, we've mastered the art of blending flavor, tradition, and flawless service.

For us, food is not just about catering — it's about creating moments that people remember long after the last bite. Whether it's a lavish wedding buffet, a corporate gala, or a satvik bhojan for sacred occasions, every menu is customized with care, hygiene, and authenticity.

And beyond business, our proudest work is serving free meals to 1,000+ nirdhans & besaharas through our social initiative — because we believe food carries not just taste, but dignity and happiness.
